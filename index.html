About Me

As a seasoned Software Engineer with over 17 years of experience, I specialize in cloud technologies, observability, and advanced data analytics. My expertise lies in designing and implementing scalable observability solutions using Microsoft Azure, KQL, Prometheus, and Power BI.

Beyond technical expertise, I have mentored teams, fostered innovation in agile environments, and driven impactful observability strategies. Passionate about leveraging cutting-edge technology, I actively engage in AI initiatives and data-driven decision-making.
